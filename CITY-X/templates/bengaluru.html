About Us

Welcome to Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the capital of Karnataka in southern India. Renowned as the "Silicon Valley of India," it is a major IT hub with a thriving tech industry and numerous startups. Founded in 1537 by Kempe Gowda, the city combines a rich historical heritage with modern advancements. Landmarks like Bangalore Palace and Vidhana Soudha highlight its cultural significance. Bengaluru is known for its pleasant climate, green spaces, and cosmopolitan culture, attracting people from across India and the world. It hosts prestigious institutions like the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB), contributing to its reputation as an educational center. The city offers a diverse culinary scene and vibrant nightlife, along with shopping districts like MG Road. While rapid urbanization brings challenges such as traffic congestion and pollution, Bengaluru remains a dynamic and influential city, driving India's technological and economic growth.
